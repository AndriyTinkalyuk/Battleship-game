Create or Join game

Battleship Game

Option 1: Create a New Room (New Room ID)
  • Enter your username in the "Username" field.
  • Click "Generate ID" to create a unique Room ID.
  • Click "Start Game" to proceed.

After creating the room:

  • You will be redirected to the game page.
  • The Room ID will be shown at the top of the screen.
  • Share this Room ID with another player using any convenient method (messenger, verbally, etc.).
Option 2: Join an Existing Room (Enter Room ID)
  • Get the Room ID from another player.
  • Enter your username in the "Username" field.
  • Enter the received Room ID in the "Enter Room ID" field.
  • Click "Start Game" to join.

After joining:

  • You will be redirected to the game page, where you will see your board and your opponent's board.
